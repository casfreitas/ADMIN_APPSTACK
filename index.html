Frequently asked questions

Here are some of the answers you might be looking for.

Does it work with Rails? Laravel? ASP.NET? Django?
Yes! We have a Django sample project, featuring allauth, debug-toolbar and crispy-forms, as well as documentation on how to use it with Pip, Pipenv and Docker To get the sample project, please reach out to us at support@bootlab.io with your order ID.

For all other frameworks (Rails, Laravel, ASP.NET, etc) we recommend including the compiled CSS/JS files directly in your application. If you want to enable deeper customization, you can integrate it into your assets pipeline or build processes. Read more here.
Can I use the static HTML, CSS and JS files?
Absolutely. You can bypass the Webpack build step completely and simply edit the static HTML, CSS and JS files included in the package.
Is there any support included?
You have access to the Software's online support services via email for six (6) months from the purchase date. Please contact us at info@bootlab.io for any questions.
How do I get a receipt for my purchase?
You'll receive an email from Bootstrap themes once your purchase is complete. More info can be found here.
What methods of payments are supported?
You can purchase the themes on Bootstrap Themes via any major credit/debit card (via Stripe) or with your Paypal account. We don't support cryptocurrencies or invoicing at this time.
